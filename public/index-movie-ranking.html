Best Movies According to Lee

My top 3 favorite movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

A man and his journey through life. This movie always makes me cry like a million times.

The Matrix

This was the first R-rated movie I ever watched. This movie had a profound effect on me and got me to start thinking outside of the box.

Spirited Away

This movie to me is about how to face your fears and to help others and forgive people who have hurt you. My 3 year old daughter also loves this movie and refers to the river spirit as ''Poo Poo Monster'' so this movie is now titled the ''Poo Poo Monster Movie''.
